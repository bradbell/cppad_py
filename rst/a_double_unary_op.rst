#############################

.. meta::

.. _a_double_unary_op@Syntax:

Syntax
******
| *ay* = + *ax*
| *ay* = - *ax*

.. meta::
   :keywords: ax

.. index:: ax

.. _a_double_unary_op@ax:

ax
**
This object has prototype

| |tab| ``const a_double&`` *ax*

.. meta::
   :keywords: ay

.. index:: ay

.. _a_double_unary_op@ay:

ay
**
If the operator is ``+`` , the result is equal to *ax* .
If it is ``-`` , the result is the negative of *ax* .
