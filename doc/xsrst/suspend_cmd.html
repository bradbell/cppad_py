suspend_cmd¶

Suspend and Resume Commands¶

  • Syntax

  • Purpose

  • Example

Syntax¶

  • {xsrst_suspend}

  • {xsrst_resume}

Purpose¶

It is possible to suspend (resume) the xsrst extraction during a section. One begins (ends) the suspension with a suspend command (resume command) at the beginning of a line. Note that this will also suspend all other xsrst processing; e.g., spell checking.
